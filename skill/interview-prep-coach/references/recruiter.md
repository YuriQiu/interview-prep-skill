# Recruiter Screen Reference

## Recruiter Goals

Recruiters usually test:

- Basic fit for the role and level.
- Motivation for the company and team.
- Communication clarity.
- Work authorization, location, start date, and logistics.
- Compensation range alignment.
- Obvious resume risks or gaps.

## Career Narrative

Use a 60-90 second structure:

1. Current role and core strength.
2. Two proof points relevant to the target role.
3. Reason for exploring this opportunity.
4. Why this company or team fits.

## Risk Handling

Handle sensitive topics truthfully and briefly:

- Career gap: explain the context, readiness, and what stayed sharp.
- Job hopping: explain the pattern and what the user wants long term.
- Layoff: keep it factual, avoid blame, pivot to fit.
- Missing requirement: acknowledge it, show adjacent evidence and learning plan.
- Compensation: ask about the range when appropriate and avoid anchoring too low.

## Closing Questions

Prepare 3-5 questions:

- What signals matter most in this interview process?
- What does success look like in the first 6 months?
- What are the biggest challenges for this team right now?
- How is the role expected to collaborate with adjacent teams?
- What are the next steps and timeline?
