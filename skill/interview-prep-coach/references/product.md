# Product Interview Reference

## Scorecard Areas

For product roles, evaluate:

- Customer/user empathy.
- Problem framing.
- Prioritization.
- Metrics and experimentation.
- Execution and stakeholder management.
- Product strategy and business judgment.
- Communication and leadership.

## Product Sense Flow

Use this structure for product design or improvement questions:

1. Clarify goal, audience, and constraints.
2. Segment users and choose a target segment.
3. Identify pain points.
4. Propose solutions.
5. Prioritize with impact, confidence, effort, and risk.
6. Define success metrics.
7. Discuss risks, tradeoffs, and launch plan.

## Execution Flow

For execution questions:

- Define the goal.
- Pick the north-star metric and guardrails.
- Diagnose drivers.
- Identify opportunities.
- Prioritize actions.
- Explain rollout and measurement.

## Strategy Flow

For strategy questions:

- State the objective.
- Describe market, customer, competition, and company strengths.
- Generate options.
- Choose a direction.
- Name risks and leading indicators.

## Common Product Questions

- Design a product for a specific user group.
- Improve a familiar product.
- Diagnose a metric drop.
- Prioritize a roadmap.
- Launch a feature.
- Decide whether to enter a market.
- Handle conflict with engineering, design, sales, or leadership.
